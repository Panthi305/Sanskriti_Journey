Places to Visit

Sabarmati Ashram

Walk through history at Sabarmati Ashram, the residence of Mahatma Gandhi. Explore the museum, see his belongings, and understand the journey of India's independence.

Manek Chowk Night Market

Visit the vibrant Manek Chowk at night for a street food experience like no other. Taste delicious local dishes like pav bhaji, chocolate sandwich, and kulfi.

Kankaria Lake

Enjoy a relaxing evening at Kankaria Lake, a perfect spot for boating, toy train rides, and visiting the zoo. A great place for families and adventure lovers alike!
