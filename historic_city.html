How to Get There

By Road

Gujarat has one of the better developed road networks in India. Ahmedabad is well connected with all major cities and towns by road. State and private buses are a great option to travel between smaller towns in the state.

By Train

Major Indian cities are also connected via the Ahmedabad railway station that lies on the western railway network. The main railway station is located in the Kalupur area. This station falls under the prominent national railway circuit and is connected to all major cities of India. India's first bullet train project will connect Mumbai to Ahmedabad by December 2023, running at a speed of 320 km/h and covering the distance in about 3 hours.

By Air

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport in Ahmedabad connects all major cities in India and key destinations abroad to the state.
